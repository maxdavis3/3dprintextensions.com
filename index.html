Printer Extension Products

01

Extruder & Hotend Mounts

Direct drive conversion kits, dual-extruder mounts, and volcano hotend adaptors for Ender 3, Prusa MK3S, Voron, and Bambu platforms. Printed in PETG and ASA for heat resistance near the hotend.

02

Cable Management & Drag Chains

Fully enclosed cable drag chains for X-axis and bed cables. Prevents fatigue failure and improves print reliability. Available in 10×10mm to 30×30mm cross-section for all printer wiring bundles.

03

Enclosures & Environmental Controls

Printer enclosure panels, door hinges, and filter housings for Ender, Prusa, and Bambu printers. HEPA + activated carbon filter mounts. Temperature-controlled enclosures for ABS and ASA printing.

UPGRADE MATERIALS

Extension Part Materials

Printer parts live in a hot, vibrating environment — material selection for upgrades matters.

  • ● PETG — heat-resistant up to 80°C, dimensionally stable
  • ● ASA — UV and chemical resistant for enclosure parts
  • ● PC — maximum heat resistance for hotend proximity parts
  • ● PLA CF — stiff, lightweight for structural brackets
  • ● TPU — vibration-damping feet and seal strips
  • ● Nylon — low-friction linear bearing and idler components
